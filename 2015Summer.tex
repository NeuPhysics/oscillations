\section{Vacuum Oscillations}

Schrodinger equation is

\begin{equation}
i\partial_t \Psi = \mathbf H \Psi,
\end{equation}

where for relativistic neutrinos, the energy is

\begin{align*}
\mathbf H &= \sqrt{p^2 + m^2} \\
&= p\sqrt{1 + \frac{m^2}{p^2}} \\
&\approx p(1 + \frac{1}{2} \frac{m^2}{p^2}).
\end{align*}
